Description

Rollaball is a logic/puzzle game, in which you have to roll down balls to get the most points.
With the balls you can manipulate arrow blocks, which change the way of the balls, making them stuck in the middle of the game field or letting them reach the bottom of the game field.
Every round gives you a unique expirence as the game field is generated randomly.

System Requirements

  • Microsoft Windows 2000 (or later)
  • DirectX 8 compatible videocard and soundcard
  • PS/2 or USB compatible mouse and keyboard
